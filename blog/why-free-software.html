Why we must insist on free software

2025-01-15 OSC-MUST en/ar

Free software is not about price — it is about freedom. The freedom to run, study, share, and modify software is a fundamental right in the digital age.

When we use proprietary software, we surrender control over our own computing to corporations whose interests rarely align with ours. We become dependent on their decisions about features, privacy, and access.

As OSC-MUST, we believe that software freedom is not a luxury — it is a necessity for education, innovation, and digital sovereignty.
