OSC MUST is a student activity community that aims to spread the FOSS in a wide spectrum of students.

Open source software is built on transparency and collaboration, allowing anyone to view, modify, and distribute code freely. It promotes decentralized development driven by community contribution and peer review.

As OSC, we reject restrictive proprietary software practices common among major technology companies and work toward making open, free, and libre software universally accessible.

As a community, we will work on Linux-based projects, organize technical workshops, and engage in hands-on initiatives that encourage practical learning and innovation.
